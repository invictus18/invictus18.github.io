Regulations:
1. Participants are not permitted to take part in more than one event.
2. It is compulsory for all participants to carry their ID card, or some other form of identification from their respective schools and have a teacher accompany them.
3. Participants must come in their school uniform and carry necessary costumes for the event with them.
4. Reporting time for both days is 8:30AM
5. Each school will be appointed a team of volunteers to assist them.
6. Decision of the judges will be final.
7. Food stalls will be spread throughout the campus and one can indulge in delicacies.
8. First aid will be provided if necessary.
9. Dates and Registration fees can be found here.
10. Last date for registration is Tuesday, 14th August 2018
11. For queries reach out to invictusdpsbangaloreeast@gmail.com

Instructions for filling out forms:
1. Enter Name, Email, Phone number and the school specific code sent in the invitation in order to register.
2. Select Event
3. Fill out details correctly according to rules given in the event pages.
4. Submit your response, and click "Add another response" to register for another event.
