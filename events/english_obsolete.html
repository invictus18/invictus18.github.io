Word Wizard(DAY1)


“Words: So innocent and powerless as they are, as standing in a dictionary, how potent for good and evil they become in the hands of one who knows how to combine them.” Nathaniel Hawthorne
Do you want to be a Vocab Wizard?
Get your dictionaries out because we are about to explore every nook and cranny of your vocabulary portal. Make sure you have your wands out and ‘spell’ings ready. Go through rounds of mind boggling and quirky challenges.
Rules
1.Each school can send one team, consisting of two participants.
2.Students of classes VIII to XII to participate.
3.The event has four rounds.
4.There will be prelims and finals, teams will qualify to the finals based on the points earned in the prior rounds.
5.Use of internet, thesaurus etc. is prohibited.
6.The first ten teams to register can participate.

Alter-Ego (Turncoat Debate)(DAY1)


"Freedom is hammered out on the anvil of discussion, dissent, and debate." - Hubert H.
Can you change colours as quick as a chameleon? Switch sides at the blink of an eye? Then, Alter Ego is the event for you. The fast paced debate requires the speaker to think on their feet as their allegiances change at the sound of a buzzer.
Rules
1. Participation – Individual Event. A maximum of 2 participants per school.
2. First 15 teams to register will be allowed to participate.
3. Duration - 2.5 minutes per participant
4. Day 1 – Prelims
Day 2 - Finals
5.Any Props/materials has to be brought by the participant.
6. Topics will be given on the spot.
7. Preparation time of 10 mins is allowed.
