Invictus

“Invictus elucidates an emotion of invincibility and indomitability. None can defeat he who wears an armour of confidence. Invictus 2018 will burn down your incapabilities and from the ashes will rise a metamorphosed you; capable, indestructible, unconquerable.

The polyglots' platoon, the cultural congregation, Invictus 2018 amasses the crème de la crème of an array of multifarious competition to nurture the best of your potential. It ignites the participants’ cultural and linguistic prowess to germinate through multilingual quizzes, singing competitions, art events, and - of course - the Question Mark Event; the signature event of Invictus.

Welcome to Invictus 2018 at Delhi Public School Bangalore East.

Be prepared to test all your knowledge in a series of unprecedented, unique contests which will build your instantaneous intelligence, rhyme and rhythm, multilingual multitude, computer capabilities and artistic academia.

Be prepared to spread your wings and fly all the way to the sun. On the 16th and 17th of August, 2018, we are no longer Icarus; we can touch the illuminating rays of the sun with our bare minds.

We are invincible, we are indomitable, we are Invictus.
